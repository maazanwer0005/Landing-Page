Amrutha Platinum Towers is strategically located in Nallurahalli, one of the most vibrant and well-connected neighbourhoods of East Bengaluru. The location offers the perfect balance of urban convenience and peaceful residential charm, ensuring a premium lifestyle for its residents. With excellent access to major IT hubs, reputed schools, hospitals, shopping destinations, and the upcoming Nallurahalli Metro Station, everything you need is just minutes away.
